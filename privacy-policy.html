Privacy Policy — Aambika Hub

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Aambika Foods & Beverages LLP ("we", "our", "us") operates the Aambika Hub mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information for plant operations management purposes:

Phone number and PIN for authentication. Employee name and role. Production data, shift reports, and machine operation data entered by operators. Maintenance work logs and spare parts usage data.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for internal plant management purposes including shift reporting, production tracking, machine monitoring, maintenance management, and operational analytics.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Google Sheets and Google Cloud infrastructure. Data is accessible only to authorized personnel within the organization.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Data is used exclusively for internal plant operations management.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. Access to the app requires authentication via registered phone number and PIN.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Sheets for data storage and Anthropic AI for maintenance troubleshooting assistance. These services have their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by employees of Aambika Foods & Beverages only. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
